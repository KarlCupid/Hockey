# Known Issues

- three-r3f chunk warning: the 3D facility remains the largest production bundle chunk.
- Generated audio is placeholder: all cues are local Web Audio tones/noise beds, not professional final audio.
- Data-pack real-world filter is basic: it catches obvious restricted terms but is not a legal guarantee.
- Mobile not primary target: small screens are supported for checking and recovery, but the beta target is desktop browser play.
- No backend/cloud: saves, feedback, telemetry, diagnostics, data packs, and reports are local/export-only.
- No professional art/audio: presentation uses CSS, primitives, generated audio, and first-party fictional branding.
- Facility art remains primitive/generated: Phase 13 improves spatial layout, districts, wayfinding, and signage, but it is not final professional 3D art or a realistic architectural simulation.
- Simplified hockey/CBA systems: waivers, buyouts, retained salary, clauses, arbitration, offer sheets, multi-team trades, online play, and playable on-ice hockey remain out of scope.
- Potential tuning needs: scoring, fatigue, story cadence, owner goals, free agency, and achievement unlock rates should be sampled during closed beta.
